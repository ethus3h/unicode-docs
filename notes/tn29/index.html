Unicode Technical Note #29

The use of Phonetic and other symbols in Dictionaries: A brief survey

Version 1
Authors Asmus Freytag
Date April 12, 2006
This Version http://www.unicode.org/notes/tn29/tn29-1.html
Previous Version n/a
Latest Version http://www.unicode.org/notes/tn29/


Summary

This Unicode Technical Note presents the result of a brief survey about the use of special symbols
to represent phonetic and other information in dictionaries. The survey intends to document
examples of typical usages, rather than provide a complete summary of existing practices.
